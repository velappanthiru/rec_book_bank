About Us

REC Book Bank was formed in the year 1999,the promoter Mr.G.RamKumar is a B.Arch from college,Chennai.His Father Mr.P.Ganesan was running tutions for higher secondary student and college student.Mr.G.RamKumar felt that there is a vast market for book bank in Chennai since the cost of books was getting higher and higher.Many students could not offer to purchase books on their own for each semester.At that time there was only one leading book bank in Chennai.Hence he has decided to start a book bank on a large scale.So REC BOOK BANK was inaugurated in the year 1999.The Present membership is around 7000. The Book Bank has employed around 12 people to look after the business.Besides Mr.Ganesan Father of the proprietor is also participating in Management decisions.Mr.RamKumar is taking care of the marketing activities and administration etc.
